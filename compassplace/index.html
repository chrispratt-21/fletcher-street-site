About

Senior living search, simplified.

Finding the right senior living community for a parent or loved one is one of the most emotionally charged, logistically complex decisions a family can face. Compass Place was built to make that process clearer, faster, and less overwhelming.

Compass Place provides personalized community matching, side-by-side comparisons, tour scheduling, and support from local Trail Guides who know the communities in your area — all at no cost to families. Our referral-based model means families always come first.

Currently serving the Hamilton County, Indiana and Columbus, Ohio areas, with expansion planned across the United States.

What Compass Place offers

🧭 Personalized Matching

Answer a few questions and get matched to communities that fit your loved one's care needs, preferences, and budget.

📊 Side-by-Side Comparisons

Compare communities across amenities, pricing, care levels, and quality scores — all in one place.

📅 Tour Scheduling

Schedule tours directly through the app. Get reminders, directions, and follow-up support.

🤝 Local Trail Guides

Connect with a local expert who knows the communities in your area and can help guide your decision.
