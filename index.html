Who We Are

A Delaware corporation building purposeful technology.

Fletcher Street Corporation is a technology holding company focused on building consumer-facing products that solve real problems for real families. We operate at the intersection of technology, trust, and human need.

Our products are built with a simple belief: the best technology disappears into the background, leaving people with more time, more confidence, and more control over their lives.

How We Build

Our principles.

01

People first

Every product decision starts with the question: does this make someone's life meaningfully better? If not, we don't build it.

02

Honest by design

We tell users exactly what we collect, why we collect it, and how it's used. No dark patterns. No buried fine print.

03

Built to last

We build for durability, not virality. Sustainable businesses that serve their communities for years, not quarters.
