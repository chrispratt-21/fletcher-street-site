The Problem

Americans drown in junk mail. There's been no good way out. Until now.

Every year, hundreds of millions of pieces of unsolicited mail land in American mailboxes — catalogs, credit offers, insurance flyers, coupon packs. Most of it goes straight to the trash. None of it was asked for. And until now, opting out has been a fragmented, frustrating, often futile process.

Pulp changes that. By turning your physical mail into structured opt-out data — verified by your address, timestamped, and categorized — we create a signal that mailers can't ignore.

Good for you. Better for the planet.

Junk mail isn't just annoying — it's an environmental problem. 100 million trees are cut down every year to produce mail that goes straight to landfill. Every piece of mail you Pulp is one less piece printed, shipped, and trashed.

Pulp tracks your environmental impact and shows you exactly how much paper, water, and carbon your opt-outs have saved.
